Creon

You are the current king of Thebes. Your brother Laius used to be king, before he was killed by a stranger while traveling. A man named Oedipus came to Thebes and solved the Sphinx's riddle and became the new king in his stead and married your sister-in-law Jocasta.

They ruled for many years until a plague struck the city and the truth came out: Oedipus was Laius and Jocasta's long-presumed-dead son and his own father's killer. Leadership of Thebes passed to his sons Polynices and Eteoles, who were unable to co-rule Thebes peacefully and launched a civil war. They destroyed each other, and you were left to pick up the pieces.
