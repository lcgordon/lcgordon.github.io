WisdomWombat

  • Wombat [Mendygral+ 2017] is an MHD simulation suite developed at UMN in the early 2010s
  • New version in development in collaboration with HPE as a demo of their new Dragon runtime environmet.
  • Directed task graph with individual processor 'agents' acting on decomposed grid elements. Creates a pseudo-shared-memory structure across distributed processes that allows for additional 'babysitter' processes to read-only analyze the data as it being produced.

    • WiserWombat

  • First generation development of that in-situ analysis.
  • PyTorch CNN to classify 2D shockfronts by angle of motion relative to x. Simple example test case for graph agent development in WisdomWombat.
  • 2022 cohort DSMMA project, paper in prep.
